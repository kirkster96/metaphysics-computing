# Privacy Policy for LocalBudget

**Effective Date:** February 17, 2026

LocalBudget is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

## 1. Local-First Approach
LocalBudget is designed as a "local-first" application. Most of your data, including your budgets, account names, and transaction history, is stored exclusively on your device. We do not maintain servers that store your personal financial data.

## 2. Bank Synchronization (Optional)
If you choose to use the bank synchronization feature, LocalBudget integrates with **SimpleFin**, a third-party data aggregator. 
- **Storage:** Your SimpleFin "Access URL" is stored securely on your device using system-provided secure storage.
- **Credentials:** We never see or store your bank credentials. All authentication happens through SimpleFin's secure bridge.
- **Data Flow:** Financial data is fetched directly from SimpleFin to your device.

## 3. Receipt Scanning (Optional)
If you use the receipt scanning feature, the processing of images and extraction of text is performed **locally on your device** using system OCR (Optical Character Recognition) capabilities (such as Apple's Vision framework or Android's ML Kit). Images of receipts are not uploaded to any external servers by LocalBudget.

## 4. No Analytics or Tracking
LocalBudget does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services. We do not collect data about how you use the application.

## 5. On-Device AI & Machine Learning Disclosure
LocalBudget uses artificial intelligence and machine learning features to enhance your experience. **All AI processing occurs entirely on your device** — no financial data, images, or personal information is ever transmitted to external servers for AI processing.

- **Receipt Scanning:** Uses Apple's Vision framework (iOS) and Google's ML Kit (Android) to extract text from receipt images. All image analysis is performed on-device using system-native capabilities.
- **Category Prediction:** Uses heuristic pattern matching to suggest transaction categories based on merchant names and descriptions. These predictions run locally using rule-based logic — no cloud-based AI models are involved.
- **No External AI Services:** LocalBudget does not connect to any third-party AI or machine learning APIs (e.g., OpenAI, Google Cloud AI, AWS). There are zero network calls related to AI functionality.

You retain full control over all AI-assisted features. Any data suggested by on-device processing — such as scanned receipt values or predicted categories — can be manually edited, overridden, or deleted at any time. AI features are optional and can be bypassed entirely through manual entry.

## 6. Data Deletion
Since your data is stored locally, you can delete all of it at any time by:
- Deleting accounts or transactions within the app.
- Clearing the application's data in your device settings.
- Uninstalling the application.
